Historic places in the V&A Waterfront

Swing Bridge

  • It was designed and constructed in 1997.
  • The bridge connects Alfred Basin and the Victoria Basin.
  • The bridge is automatic but can be manually operated in the event of failure.

Portswood Tunnel

  • This tunnel was build to move stone from the quarry to the breakwater for building the harbour.
  • The tunnel was build in 1860..
  • This meuseum showcases maritime history of Table Bay.

Clock Tower

  • The Clock Tower acted as the original Port Captain's office.
  • The construction of the Clock Tower was completed in 1882.
  • The second floor is a decorative mirror room, where the Port Captian would check on all the activities of the harbour.
